- Rewrote `InteractiveParticles.tsx` from orbital circles to **confetti dashes** (small rotated rounded rectangles)

- "Get Started" and "Sign In" buttons trigger a 500ms fade-out + scale(0.97) + blur(6px) before navigation

  - **Left panel**: logo (0.1s) → badge (0.3s) → headline (0.5s) → subtitle (0.7s) → pills (0.9s) → tagline (1.1s)
